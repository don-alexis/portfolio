Compositing

Role: Image retouching and compositing

These are a series of personal works that I created to demonstrate some compositing and digital imaging techniques using photoshop.

The first composition shows a couple on a canoe rowing up a building trying to reach the pristine waters on the top.

The second composition is herd of elephants rampaging through Buckingham palace.

The third composition is similar to the first, but I used a horse race instead.

The last composition is an illustration of heavenly bodies that are cubicle and covered with water.
