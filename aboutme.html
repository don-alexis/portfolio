Don-Alexis R. Martirez


Don Martirez is a consummate problem solver, investigator, artist, and creative beast that has completely lost all sense of reality. For the past couple of years, Don’s been completely immersed in his study of design- losing all sense of balance and social life. “What do you want me to say?- I love this stuff !”; is the usual reply he’ll give you.


He's in his last year of studying at Mt. Sierra College and he's looking forward to the next chapter of his design career. Having been formally trained with a foundational design curriculum that involves digital imaging, illustration, storytelling, interactive media, and designing software applications for the web- he is excited for the opportunities that lie ahead.


He is comfortable working without a WYSISWG and can hand roll code for HTML, CSS3, Javascript/Jquery and PHP. He has a working knowledge base that includes responsive design, motion graphics, designing for mobile devices, AJAX, UX/UI, and progressive enhancement.


Don is looking for opportunites that will use his design education and propagate a career in software design and development. He has a unique imagination, is highly determined, possesses bottomless creative energy, and is always anxious to learn new skills.
