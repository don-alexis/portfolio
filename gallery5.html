Mount Auburn Hospital

Designer: Hospital Graphics System

Mount Auburn Hospital is a hospital in Cambridge, Massachusetts, affiliated with Harvard Medical School. It was founded in 1886 as the first hospital in Cambridge and until 1947 was known as Cambridge Hospital.

Mount Auburn Hospital wanted some simple designs for various marketing collateral. This project involved creating designs for a brochure, a buckslip, postcards, letterhead, envelopes, a tri-fold brochure, and medical forms. I took their existing logo and color pallet to create designs that align themselves to what can be found in hospital graphic systems.
